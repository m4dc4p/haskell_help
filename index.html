Call me at The Haskell Helpline to get a little help with your Haskell. Each caller gets up to 15 minutes of my time. Calls will not be recorded but I will be posting about them. I will leave out as much personally identifiable information as I can, but I can't make any guarantees. There is no charge for this service.

In order to have the best experience, please follow these guidelines:

  • Have example code posted online. I suggest using GitHub's Gists or HPaste. You can also at haskell_help@codeslower.com.
  • Prepare a minimal working example when possible.
  • I can't answer platform-specific questions (unless they are about Windows), though I am happy to guess.
